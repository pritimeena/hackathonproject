Popular destinations in Delhi

Delhi, a city of contrasts, Where history and modernity coalesce. A chaotic charm that never rests.

  • Delhi

    India Gate

    The India Gate is a war memorial located near the Kartavya path on the eastern edge of the "ceremonial axis" of New Delhi, formerly called duty path.

  • Delhi

    Humayun’s Tomb

    Humayun's tomb is the tomb of Humayun, the second emperor of the Mughal Empire, in Delhi, India.

  • Delhi

    Red Fort

    The Red Fort, the largest monument in Delhi, is one of its most popular tourist destinations and attracts thousands of visitors every year.

  • Delhi

    Agrasen Ki Baoli

    Agrasen Ki Baoli is a 60-meter long and 15-meter wide historical stepwell in New Delhi, India.

  • Delhi

    Lotus Temple

    The Lotus Temple, located in New Delhi, India. Is a Bahá'iʼ house of worship that was dedicated in December 1986.

  • Delhi

    Akshardham

    'Akshardham' means the divine abode of God. It is hailed as an eternal place of devotion, purity and peace.
